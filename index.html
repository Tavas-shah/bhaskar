Bhaskarprabha Foundation

"Bhaskarprabha Gurukula, founded on May 21, 2023 by Shri Tejas Kulkarni, offers a unique education experience focused on Bhartiya ganit (Indian mathematics). It is the first traditional gurukul-style school dedicated to the study of ganit (mathematics). Rooted in the traditional Bhartiya education system, it aims to revive and preserve ancient Bhartiya ganit while also incorporating modern mathematics, statistics, and computer science. Located in Pune, Maharashtra, the gurukula strives to provide students with a comprehensive, value-based education that blends traditional wisdom with contemporary knowledge."
